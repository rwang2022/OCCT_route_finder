Tutorial/FAQ

If your question is not here, click Suggestions at the top and email me.


What's the lock about?

The lock is a way for you to fix the time difference between the start and end time.

What do the stops mean?

The stops are the official names of the stops in the OCCT bus pdf.
Some examples include "Leaves UDC", "Returns to Campus", and "Arrives at UDC".

Yes, this does mean that you need to know what your stop is called for this app to be useful.

What are the 3 buttons for?

Those 3 buttons are there for conveniently setting the 3 most common trips.
